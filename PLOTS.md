## About the Data

These visualizations are generated from official Tesouro Direto data, providing insights into the Brazilian government bond market. The data includes:

- **Demographics**: Investor profile information
- **Operations**: Trading volumes and market activity
- **Prices**: Daily pricing and yield information for all available securities
- **Stock**: Outstanding positions by security type

For more information about Tesouro Direto, visit the [official website](https://www.tesourodireto.com.br/).
